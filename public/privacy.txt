===== Privacy Policy =====

This document governs the way in which Tanksmith.io ("We" or "Us") collects information from its users (each, a "User") within the website tanksmith.io (the "Site"). This policy applies to all services provided by the Site.

=== Collection of information ===

We collect information, directly and via third parties, to enhance the quality of the Site. The User may voluntarily provide a non-personal means of identification (their "Name") to be used temporarily within the Site.

=== Our use of the information ===

The information collected, not including the User's Name, is aggregated into general statistics, describing the usage of all the Site's Users, in our application of the data. We never collect the User's personal information involuntarily, or associate that User's personal information or usage patterns with their personal identity.

=== Interaction with other users ===

Other Users of the Site may see the User's Name. We have no control over the Site's other Users' behavior in conjunction with this information.

=== Our storage of the information ===

To enhance the User's experience, We may store their Name and preferences locally on their machine. This information is never transmitted or permanently recorded remotely.

=== Third parties ===

We may use third-party Service Providers to show advertisements to you to help support and maintain the Site. Depending on the ads that are served, your data may be shared with any of the ad technology providers listed below. Ad technology providers (including Google, Ad Exchanges, SSPs, DSPs, ad networks, bidders and vendors) use your data for example, to show you personalised ads or to report on conversions. In the overview below you can find links to information on how the technology providers use your data. More information is made available by each individual ad provider in the "Ad providers" section below.

=== Changes to this document ===

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time, without notifying the User. Changes will always be available on this page in the Site.

=== The User's acceptance of the policy ===

By using this Site, the User accepts the terms of this Policy. If the User does not agree to the terms of this document, the User may discontinue association with the Site.

This policy was last updated on April 14th, 2018
